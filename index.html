Meet Your Instructor

Hi, I'm
Charlene

I'm a yoga instructor based in the Salt Lake City area. I believe yoga is for every body — whether you're flowing through a heated vinyasa or finding your first downward dog.

My classes are energetic, inclusive, and community-driven. From hot yoga and power flows to partner yoga workshops, I love creating spaces where people push their boundaries while discovering what their bodies can do. Come sweat, breathe, and grow with me.

"Get your yoga on. Everyone is welcome."
